Headmaster Message

"शिक्षा ही जीवन का मूल आधार है।"

प्रिय अभिभावकों, छात्रों और शुभचिंतकों,

मैं अत्यंत गर्व और हर्ष के साथ आप सभी का PRIMARY SCHOOL HARI MUSHAR TOL, BATHNAHA, SITAMARHI, BIHAR में स्वागत करता हूँ। हमारा विद्यालय न केवल शैक्षणिक उत्कृष्टता की दिशा में प्रयासरत है, बल्कि बच्चों के सर्वांगीण विकास हेतु एक सकारात्मक, सुरक्षित और प्रेरणादायक वातावरण प्रदान करने के लिए समर्पित है।

हमारा उद्देश्य विद्यार्थियों को ज्ञान के साथ-साथ संस्कार, अनुशासन और नैतिक मूल्यों से युक्त करना है ताकि वे आने वाले समय में एक जिम्मेदार नागरिक बन सकें। शिक्षकगण पूरी निष्ठा और लगन से बच्चों की शिक्षा में योगदान दे रहे हैं।

मैं सभी अभिभावकों से अनुरोध करता हूँ कि वे विद्यालय के साथ सहयोग बनाए रखें और बच्चों को शिक्षित और सशक्त बनाने के इस मिशन में भागीदार बनें।

हमारा विश्वास है कि यदि विद्यालय, शिक्षक, अभिभावक और छात्र मिलकर कार्य करें, तो हम हर बच्चे के भविष्य को उज्जवल बना सकते हैं।

School Introduction

About School | Our Mission | Our Vision | Our Values
Primary School Hari Mushar Tol, Bathanaha, Sitamarhi, Bihar is a government-recognized institution dedicated to providing quality foundational education to young learners. As a state-organized primary school, it serves as a pillar of inclusive learning, especially for students from rural and underprivileged backgrounds. With a strong focus on literacy, values, discipline, and overall development, the school nurtures students in a safe and encouraging environment. Our dedicated teachers work passionately to ensure that each child receives equal opportunities to grow academically, socially, and emotionally. We aim not only to educate but to empower — building a future where every child is equipped with the knowledge, confidence, and compassion needed to succeed.

Our Department

CLASS-I

Class I lays the foundation for scientific curiosity by introducing young learners to basic concepts of nature and the world around them through interactive and fun experiments.

CLASS-II

Class II nurtures creativity and imagination through exposure to various forms of arts, including storytelling, painting, music, and drama to develop expressive skills.

CLASS-III

Class III introduces the basics of commerce and trade, teaching students the importance of money, markets, and simple economic activities through age-appropriate lessons.

CLASS-IV

Class IV focuses on strengthening mathematical skills by exploring numbers, shapes, and patterns, encouraging logical thinking and problem-solving abilities.

CLASS-V

Class V explores historical events and stories, helping students understand their heritage, culture, and the importance of learning from the past.

TEACHERS CURRICULUM

Our Teachers' Curriculum incorporates modern educational techniques and technology, designed to equip educators with innovative tools to enhance classroom learning.
